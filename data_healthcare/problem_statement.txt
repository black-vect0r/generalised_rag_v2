Problem Statement: Healthcare providers often face challenges in managing patient appointments and follow-ups efficiently, leading to missed appointments, delayed treatments, and poor patient engagement. Manual scheduling and follow-up processes are time-consuming and prone to errors. Steps: Develop an AI agent that interacts with patients to schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments using natural language. Integrate the agent with existing healthcare scheduling systems or simulate scheduling data. Enable the agent to send automated reminders and follow-up messages to patients based on appointment dates. Allow the agent to answer common patient queries related to appointments, preparation instructions, and post-visit care. Test the agent’s ability to handle multiple patient interactions concurrently within the hackathon timeframe. Data Requirements: Sample patient appointment data including patient names, appointment dates, and types of visits; common patient queries and responses related to appointment management; simulated calendar or scheduling system data for integration. Expected Output: An interactive AI agent capable of managing appointment scheduling and follow-ups via conversational interface, producing logs of interactions and appointment status updates in JSON or tabular format
